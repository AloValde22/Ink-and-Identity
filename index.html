About Ink & Identity

Ink & Identity is a creative space dedicated to celebrating independent female authors through storytelling, branding, and design. Each page explores the connection between a writer’s voice and the visual identity that brings her story to life, offering inspiration, resources, and thoughtful examples of book branding.
